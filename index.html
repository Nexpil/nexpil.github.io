The Future of Intelligient Disease Management

The nexpil system is a patented solution that intelligently and automatically assigns colored shapes to each of the patents medications and chronic disease management tasks. When it is time for the patient to perform a specific task (like take their medication or check their blood pressure, etc.) a colored shape that represents that task is displayed on a nexpil device or the patient’s smart phone.
